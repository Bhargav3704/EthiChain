Our Solution

AI-Powered Certificate Verification

Our system leverages web scraping to verify the authenticity of sustainability certificates. Users can also upload certificates, which are securely stored and analyzed using the Gemini API to ensure compliance and detect inconsistencies.

Blockchain-Powered Transparency

Verified claims are stored on an immutable ledger, ensuring no tampering.

QR Code-Based Verification
